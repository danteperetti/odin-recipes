Issa Pasta Ting!

Follow along to make your own Pasta Ting!

Anybody can make a Pasta Ting.

The question is how bad do you want it?

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Sauce

Steps:

  1. Put da noodles in hot water.
  2. Take da soft noodles out of hot water.
  3. Put da sauce on da noodles.
  4. Have Fun!
